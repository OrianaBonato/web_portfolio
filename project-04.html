Rocío García Psicóloga

When I first spoke with Rocío, she told me she needed a website that explained her work, experience, and services. But the challenge went beyond information: the site needed to express who she is. Even though the content included a fair amount of explanatory text about her approach, it was essential that it never felt clinical or distant — as a psychologist, her professional expertise had to come through clearly, but always wrapped in warmth.

We built her brand from the ground up: designing the logo, choosing a palette of terracotta and blush tones that evoke calm and trust, and selecting imagery that reflects care and active listening. The result is a website that pairs elegant typography with soft, warm photography — creating a digital space that feels as safe and human as her therapy room.
